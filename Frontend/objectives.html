Our achievements

The aims and objectives of the VSP are to:

1.To promote and foster the unity, political stability, equality and national consciousness of the citizens of Kenya.
2.To promote true devolution in the Republic of Kenya.
3.To setup branches of the Party throughout the Republic of Kenya and beyond and recruit members to the Party.
4.To sponsor eligible candidates and drum up support for votes for election into all elective offices in all tiers of government.
5.To consciously pursue the implementation of the policies and programs of the Party, members that are through those of its appointed or elected into government, legislative houses and Boards throughout the Country.
